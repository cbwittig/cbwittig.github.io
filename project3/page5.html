Word Cloud: Reference & User Services Quarterly (volumes 52-54)

Visualizations

This illustration shows the most frequently used terms in three volumes (52-54) of the journal Reference & User Services Quarterly. This is a sampling of what I will produce across the full run of each of the journals in my study

My research identifies patterns, trends, and a network of concepts (topic modeling) from tables of contents, abstracts, and text mining (searching through keywords of entire journal runs). Text mining will provide key term frequency lists and this analysis will identify in what journals the concepts were discussed by authors and where (or if) discussions of the terms/concepts occurred over time. These methods will allow me to identify the density of terms over the entire run of the journal through 2018 and map a timespan to analyze how these concepts have been taken up over the last century.
